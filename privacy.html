Privacy Policy

This policy explains how NEXUS collects, uses, shares, and protects information when users interact with the platform.

Policy Summary

NEXUS operates as an independent auto insurance comparison platform.

We help users compare providers, explore coverage options, and request quotes across the United States. We do not provide insurance policies or act as an insurance provider.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

NEXUS ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our platform.

NEXUS is an independent auto insurance comparison platform that helps drivers compare providers, review coverage options, and request quotes across the United States. We do not provide insurance policies or services directly.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you agree to be bound by the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • fill out contact forms
  • request quotes
  • communicate with us
  • participate in surveys

This may include:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • ZIP code and location
  • vehicle information
  • coverage preferences
  • and any other information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • IP address
  • location data
  • browser type and version
  • operating system
  • pages visited and time spent
  • referring websites
  • device information

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • connecting you with relevant insurance providers
  • communication
  • marketing (with consent)
  • analytics
  • legal compliance
  • fraud prevention and platform security

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • insurance providers relevant to your request
  • service providers
  • legal authorities when required
  • in business transfers
  • with your consent

All partners are expected to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including secure connections, controlled access, and regular assessments. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • access
  • correct
  • delete
  • opt-out
  • object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13.

8. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for third-party websites.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under CCPA.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country.

11. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time.
